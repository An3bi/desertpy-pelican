Lightning Talks

Lightning talks are short (~5-10 minute) presentations on small, often beginner-friendly topics. When possible, these talks are tacked onto the end of our regular meetings. Periodically we may also host a Thunderstorm, a meeting consisting entirely of lightning talks.

Here we will maintain a list of requested topics for lightning talks. Comment on the lightning talk topic list if have any topic suggestions! Likewise, let us know if you are interested in covering one of these topics at an upcoming meeting.
